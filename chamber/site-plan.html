Euphony GH Music Group - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Name:Euphony GH

Reason:"Euphony" means harmonious sound, which reflects the identity of the group. The name is elegant, fitting for a contemporary chorale ensemble

2. Site purpose

The website will showcase the Euphony music group, providing:

  • Member bios and photos
  • A performance gallery with images
  • A contact form for inquiries and auditions
  • Dynamic content loaded ia JavaScript from a JSON file
  • Local storge to remember user preferences

3. Scenarios

  • "How can i join or audition for Eupphony GH
  • "Where can i view past performances or listen to song previews?"

4. Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5) - used for headings and navigation
  • Accent Color: Coral (#FF6F61) - used for buttons and highlights
  • Background Color: Soft Gray (#333333) - used for body text

5. Color Scheme Use

Example usage:

Welcome to Euphony GH

Discover the harmony of voices and the beauty of contemporary chorale music.

6. Typography

  • Heading Font:Montserrat - used for titles and section headers
  • Body Font:Open Sans - used for paragraphs and general content
  • Accent Font:Playfair Display - used for quotes and song titles
